# Privacy Policy for YouTube Video Saver

**Last Updated:** May 16, 2026

Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how **YouTube Video Saver** (the "Extension") handles information when you use our software.

### 1. Information Collection and Use

YouTube Video Saver is designed to help you locally log and manage your YouTube browsing activity.

* **What We Collect:** When you navigate YouTube, the Extension parses public video metadata from the webpage, including the YouTube video identifier (`videoId`), video title, channel name, video duration, and a timestamp of when it was saved.
* **Purpose:** This data is captured solely to compile your personal history log and display it cleanly on your local history dashboard page (`history.html`).

### 2. Data Storage and Local Processing

We believe in absolute data ownership.

* **100% Local Storage:** All captured data, video history, blacklist preferences, and extension settings are stored strictly on your local device using the secure `chrome.storage.local` API.
* **No Cloud / Remote Servers:** The Extension **does not** transmit, sync, upload, or share any of your data, browsing habits, or history records to external cloud servers, remote databases, or third-party entities.

### 3. Permissions Justification

To fulfill its single purpose, the Extension requires the following permissions:

* **`storage`**: Used to save your video log items and layout configurations locally on your machine.
* **`tabs`**: Used to safely launch your local history control panel screen in a new browser tab when clicking the toolbar icon.
* **`*://www.youtube.com/*`**: Allows the extension to run content scripts to read open public video parameters exclusively while you are browsing YouTube.

### 4. Third-Party Disclosures and Analytics

* **No Tracking:** We do not use third-party analytics hooks, telemetry toolkits, or advertising SDKs.
* **No Data Selling:** We do not sell, trade, or rent user data to any third party under any circumstances. Your data never leaves your browser context.

### 5. Data Control and Deletion

You maintain complete control over your history data. You can erase your data at any time directly through the Extension's interface by:

* Deleting individual video entries.
* Clearing your entire history collection using the "Blacklist All" or trash management buttons.
* Uninstalling the extension, which automatically prompts Chrome to wipe all local data caches associated with it.

### 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated modification date.

### 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this privacy policy or the practices of the Extension, please contact us via our developer support channel on the Chrome Web Store page.
