### Privacy Policy: GitHub to GitHub.io

**Last Updated:** May 24, 2026

This extension is designed for transparency and user privacy.

* **What data we collect:** We do not collect, store, or share any personal data, browsing history, or user information.
* **How we use permissions:** We use the `activeTab` permission solely to read the URL of the GitHub repository page you are currently viewing. This allows the extension to perform a single, user-initiated redirect to the corresponding GitHub Pages URL.
* **Your Control:** The extension only runs when you manually click the icon. It has no background access to your data and does not track your activity across other websites.
* **Third Parties:** We do not share information with any third-party services.
